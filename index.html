my peak of dyslexia pain
(chapter one)

I am 12 y.o. and i am in love with my classmate. she didn't notice me for a long time, but one day she accidentally began to message me. i was happy, but suffered. i covered in sweat each time i got a message from her. i was really scared to seem illiterate, my replies were monosyllabic, though I wanted much more. lord, even now these dry answers humiliate me in my own eyes.

after a few weeks our communication faded, but my feelings did not. later I asked why and she told me that she felt lack of interest in my answers. returning home i sobbed bitterly
